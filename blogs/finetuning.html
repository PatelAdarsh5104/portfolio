Mastering Model Fine-Tuning

Published on April 15, 2024

Fine-tuning is the process of taking a pre-trained model and training it further on a smaller, specific dataset to adapt it for a particular task.

When to Fine-Tune?

You should consider fine-tuning when RAG isn't enough, especially when you need the model to learn a specific style, tone, or highly specialized terminology that isn't present in the base model.

Techniques

Common techniques include Full Fine-Tuning, where all parameters are updated, and Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning (PEFT) like LoRA, which only updates a small subset of weights.
